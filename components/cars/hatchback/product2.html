Fordon's Finest Chevrolet Spark
Chevrolet Spark
₱850,000.00

The Chevrolet Spark is Chevrolet’s entry to the thriving subcompact hatchback segment in the Philippines. Its refreshing exterior, interior, and price range make this car a good choice for a first brand-new car. The signature almond-shaped headlights of the Spark give it a unique look, together with the two-door hatch illusion because of the placement of the rear door handles.

Inside, the Chevrolet Spark has a clean dashboard layout, employing smaller knobs and buttons but illuminated it with LED lighting for easier control in the dark. A 7-inch touchscreen display with Chevrolet MyLink system decorates the center console and can be controlled using the buttons on the steering wheel. On the other hand, a traditional 3-inch LCD display on the instrument cluster replaced the motorcycle-inspired gauge on the previous model.

Enjoy the Spark with a 1.4L gasoline engine that comes with either a 5-speed manual transmission or CVT. The engine produces up 99 hp and 128 Nm of torque.

Fordon's Finest Lexus LC
Lexus LC
₱850,000.00

The LC is Lexus flagship in the local market's coupe segment. The classy Japanese four-seater coupe features the the brand's spindle grille in 3D mesh, complemented by a set of L-shape daytime running lights on the headlamps. This, along with its low-on-ground stance, brag about the brand's true identity. All 4 passengers are to be greeted by high-grade leather and an Alcantara-wrapped cabin, from the steering wheel, all the way to the seats. To further add class, Lexus accentuates these with metal trims found all over the cockpit, which houses the 10.3-inch Electro Multi-Vision Display that shows the vehicle information and entertainment features, and is supported by 13 surrounding speakers. Power-wise, a 5.0 naturally-aspirated V8 engine is found under the hood, which feeds the car with 471 hp and 50 Nm of torque. This thunderous engine is coupled to a 10-speed automatic transmission, giving the car the ability to go from 0 to 100 km/h in 4.7 seconds.
